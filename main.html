Our Products

Mapwell&trade is our Financial Planning Platform that organizes and aggregates fragmented information to empowers Financial Advisors toward building next generation 'Practice Management'.


Mapwell addresses serious current gaps in the Financial Services Industry and aims to provide holistic, timely and integrated information to end-users. It leverages existing technologies and combines them powerfully to provide one-stop solution to Financial Advisors

The Top Funds (ETFs) Model demonstrates this information by deploying the power of our intangibles (investment algorithms) with the power of cloud super-computing.


We aim to separate signals from noise and organize the Top Funds data efficiently, thereby saving time and costs to Banks and Wall Street companies. Our disruptive model aims to shake the stagnant Assets Industry.
